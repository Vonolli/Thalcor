O’LIXIR is a consumer product laboratory for taste-forward energy, precise formulation, and a darker premium brand system.

## Direction

The work begins with formulation, naming, packaging language, and market taste tests. The goal is not another loud energy drink. The goal is a controlled, memorable object with chemistry that earns repeat behavior.

## Current questions

- Which functional ingredients can support clean energy without noise?
- What flavor profile feels premium instead of novelty-driven?
- How should a beverage brand behave when it refuses generic hype?
