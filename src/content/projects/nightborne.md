Nightborne is the creative infrastructure track for audiovisual worlds, music identity, live expression, and mythic media systems.

## Direction

The project treats media as an engine rather than a campaign. Visual language, sound design, story fragments, and release rituals are designed to reinforce one another over time.

## Current questions

- What symbols can carry a world without over-explaining it?
- How can releases feel like artifacts rather than content?
- Which production systems make cinematic quality repeatable?
